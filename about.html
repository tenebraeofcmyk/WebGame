About:


This text based adventure game was designed to evaluate students' abilities in an engaging manner to hold student attention.

Basically to serve as a learning tool without feeling like a learning tool!

Key fetaures include:

  • Various Puzzles to Challenge the Target Grade Level
  • Interesting Story Line to Keep Players Engaged
  • Seamless Integration Between Interactive Rooms
